Making a website for Royal Giant High School

## Introduction

Royal Giant High School is a prestigious educational institution located in Mityana, Uganda. We are committed to providing quality education and fostering a nurturing environment for our students to thrive academically and personally.

Our school offers a wide range of academic programs, extracurricular activities, and sports to ensure that our students receive a well-rounded education. We believe in empowering our students to become responsible citizens and leaders of tomorrow.

## Website Design

The website design should be modern, user-friendly, and visually appealing. The website should have a clear and concise layout, with easy navigation and intuitive design. The website should also be responsive, meaning it should look good on all devices, including desktops, tablets, and smartphones.

## Website Content

The website should include the following sections:

- Home: This section should provide an overview of the school, including its history, mission, and values. It should also include a brief description of the school's academic programs and extracurricular activities.

- About: This section should provide more detailed information about the school, including its history, mission, and values. It should also include information about the school's academic programs and extracurricular activities.

- Academics/Programs: This section should provide information about the school's academic programs, including the curriculum, requirements, and expectations for students.

- Gallery: This section should provide a collection of images and photos of the school's facilities, students, and staff. It should also include information about the school's extracurricular activities and sports.

- Contact: This section should provide contact information for the school, including its address, phone number, email, and social media links.

## Website Functionality

The website should be fully functional, including the ability to search for specific information, such as academic programs, extracurricular activities, and sports. It should also include a payment gateway for students to purchase school supplies and uniforms.

## Website Maintenance

The website should be regularly updated with the latest information about the school, including academic programs, extracurricular activities, and sports. It should also be regularly maintained to ensure that it is secure and up-to-date.

## Website Optimization
